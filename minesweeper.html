Minesweeper Instructions:

You are presented with a board full of squares. Some of these squares contain mines and others don't. The squares that don't contain mines contain the number of how many surrounding squares are mines.

If you click on a square containing a bomb, you lose and must restart. If you manage to click all the squares without clicking on a bomb, you win.

Good Luck!
