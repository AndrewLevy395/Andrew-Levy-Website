Color Converter Instructions:

Whenever two blocks of the same color collide, they transform into a new color. The colors tranform in the order of blue, purple, dark green, light green, yellow, orange, red, pink, gray, brown, and then black.

The pieces can be moved around using the arrow keys. When a key is pressed, all of the pieces on the board are shifted in that direction until they reach the edge of the board or are obstructed by another piece.

The goal of the game is to create a black block before the board is completely filled up with squares. The number of remaining colors is displayed to the right of the board.

Good Luck!
