All About Me

School: Before college I attended Cheshire High School (CHS) in Cheshire, Connecticut. At CHS I was a member of the Latin Club and was the captain of the men's track team. In 2016, I graduated with an honor cord in career and technical education. Currently I attend Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) in Worcester, Massachusetts. I am working towards receiving a bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a minor in Interactive Media and Game Development. I also have taken several humanities classes with a focus on art and digital imaging. On campus I am a member of Beta Theta Pi, participate in several intramural sports leagues, and will ocassionally attend meetings at WPI's popular Cheese Club.

Hobbies and Interests: Ever since coming to college I have fallen in love with programming. My high school did not offer much oppurtunity for learning to code, however during my time in college, programming has become one of my favorite hobbies. I also enjoy playing and watching sports. In high school I ran track and was the captain of my team. I am a huge fan of the Cincinnati Bengals and the Orlando Magic (despite living in Massachusetts). At WPI, I am a brother of the Eta Tau chapter of Beta Theta Pi. Beta is a group of brothers who strive to push each other to become better men by following the character values of Mutual Assistance, Intellectual Growth, Trust, Responsible Conduct, and Integrity. Lastly, to destress from school and work I enjoy playing video games. Ever since I was a kid I have loved everything about video games and have been interested in how they work, as well as the game development process. Because of this interest, I am working towards receiving a game development minor in my studies.
