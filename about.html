Interactive Qualifying Project: In the fall of my junior year I traveled abroad to Switzerland for a term to work on an Interactive Qualifying Project (IQP). An IQP is a group based research project hosted by WPI. The goal of my project was to improve accessibility at the Grabser Muhlbäch, a privately owned outdoor museum located in Grabs. Our final deliverable for the project was a manual with a list of recommendations that the Grabser Muhlbäch Association on several improvements they could make. Some of these changes include: adding QR Codes to easily access information, redesigning their website, improved navigation with Google MyMaps integration, as well as more.
